Roles#

VRParty has a few roles that can be used to Identify staff, official bots, and Donators.

Roles:

  • VRP Owner: The role designated to the Owner of VRParty, Reno.

  • Admin: This is a super user role, users with this role can do almost everything the Owner can and assist with running VRParty.

  • Moderator: The mod Role is for users who help keep VRP clean of malicious users and keep bad content off the Instance.

  • VRP Official: This Role is for bots on VRP, if you see a bot without this role then it is not an official bot.

  • VRP Supporter: This Role is for users who support VRP financially on Patreon ‚ù§Ô∏è.
